CONTROLS
--------------

AD - Move side to side

W - Jump/ Climb ladder

SPACE - aim beam OR move platform that you are standing on

LEFT CLICK - Shoot projectile while aiming

E - pick up note

T - Toggle reflective mirror
Move mouse to postion the mirror
Use mouse scroll to control the range

Left click to place it down and then move the mouse to rotate it

Left click again to place it

Right click to destroy it

R - restart level


OBSTACLES
-------------

GREEN - ladders to climb

LIGHT BLUE - Movable platform

DARK BLUE OUTLINE - solar panel that can be hit with light

YELLOW - Elevator with a small button to activate it

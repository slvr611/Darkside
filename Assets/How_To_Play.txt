CONTROLS
--------------

AD - Move side to side

W - Jump/ Climb ladder

SPACE - aim beam OR move platform that you are standing on

LEFT CLICK - Shoot projectile while aiming

E - pick up note

T - Toggle reflective mirror

R - restart level


OBSTACLES
-------------

GREEN - ladders to climb

LIGHT BLUE - Movable platform

DARK BLUE OUTLINE - solar panel that can be hit with light

PINK - Elevator with a small button to activate it
